Admin Roles

From November 2018 to March 2022, I worked in administration at a high-end retail store, starting as an Accounts Assistant and Receptionist. In this role, I was the first point of contact for customers and suppliers both in-store and over the phone, directing calls and providing high-quality customer service in a fast-paced environment. I also managed the store’s banking, including handling payments and refunds, and used Excel to track daily transactions and sales. Through these roles, I developed strong communication, organisational, and problem-solving skills, as well as proficiency in data management and customer service.
